Tomato_Bacterial_spot
Symptoms:
Symptoms of bacterial leaf spot may include black edged lesions, brown spots with yellow halos, or just light and dark areas on the foliage.
Symptoms of bacterial leaf spot may also appear on the edges of a leaf, where it appears brownish yellow and the tissue dries and breaks off.
The leaves become quite papery and delicate when the bacterial disease attacks the leaf edges.
Preventive Measures:
choose disease-free transplants and rotate crops.
avoid overhead watering to prevent spreading the bacteria.
Remove old vegetable debris in the garden and do not plant new crops where host plants were once growing.
